Every course needs a reason. Ours has a gate. 每一門課都需要一個理由。我們的，有一道校門。

Walk out of our school gate and the wider world is already there: a high-speed-rail station three minutes away, a national museum of Asian art, a town named after a Qing admiral’s title. Younger children learn those facts. This course asks something more of our teenagers: can you stand in front of a visitor and explain your own town, in English? Not a textbook dialogue about an imaginary city — the real gate, the real museum, the real station, named and explained in a second language by the students who live beside them.

走出我們的校門，更大的世界早已在那裡：三分鐘外的高鐵站、一座國家級的亞洲藝術博物館、一個以清代提督封號命名的小鎮。小小孩學這些事實。這門課對我們的青少年要求得更多：你能不能站在一位訪客面前，用英文介紹自己的家鄉？不是課本裡一座虛構城市的對話——而是真實的校門、真實的博物館、真實的車站，由住在它們旁邊的學生，用第二種語言叫出名字、講出故事。

The best place to learn a second language is the place you already know best in the first one. 學第二種語言最好的地方，是你早已用第一種語言最熟悉的那個地方。

Classroom Resources

English for Every Classroom 把英語帶進每一間教室

Beyond the signature course, our teachers draw on two ready-made bilingual playlists — the everyday classroom phrases and the school-announcement language that bring English into daily campus life.

在特色課程之外，老師們還用兩個現成的雙語影音 playlist——把課堂上每天會用到的句子、以及校園廣播會用到的語言，帶進校園日常。

Resource 1 of 2 · Bilingual Toolkit

Classroom English 課室英語 · 老師上課的英文工具箱

Ten episodes by Sarah Thomas — the classroom phrases every bilingual teacher reaches for, from morning roll call to wrap-up. Taibao’s teachers can use this playlist as a personal warm-up before an English-medium lesson.

由 Sarah Thomas 老師主講的 10 集系列——把雙語老師在課堂上每天會用到的句子，從早晨點名一路收進收尾活動。太保的老師可以把它當作上英語課前的個人暖身。

10 episodes Host: Sarah Thomas Topics: greeting · prep · explanation · pre-activity · feedback · management · activity · assignment · wrap-up · assessment
Resource 2 of 2 · Bilingual Toolkit

Bilingual Announcements 廣播英語 · 校園廣播的雙語版

Thirteen bilingual episodes by Sarah Thomas and Susan Rose: each of a school’s four offices — academic affairs, student affairs, general affairs, and counselling — introducing themselves in two languages. Play it over the morning intercom, or watch it in homeroom.

由 Sarah Thomas 與 Susan Rose 兩位老師雙語主講，共 13 集——四個處室（教務、學務、總務、輔導）分別用兩種語言自我介紹。可以放在早晨校園廣播，也可以在班會時間播放。

13 episodes Hosts: Sarah Thomas · Susan Rose Offices: Academic Affairs (5) · Student Affairs (3) · General Affairs · Counselling
