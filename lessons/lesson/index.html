Young Guides of Taibao · Lesson 1

Welcome to My Town 第一課 · 歡迎來到我的家鄉

Greet a visitor, and explain how our town got its unusual name. 歡迎一位訪客，並解釋我們的家鄉如何得到這個不尋常的名字。

By the end of this lesson, you can: welcome a visitor in English, say your name and school, and explain in one or two sentences how Taibao got its name.

這堂課結束時，你能：用英文歡迎一位訪客、說出自己的名字與學校、並用一兩句話解釋太保這個地名的由來。
1

The Situation 情境 · 你的任務

A group of guests has just arrived at your school — maybe a sister school from another county, maybe visitors from abroad. Your teacher turns to you and says, “You be the guide.” The first thing a good guide does is simple: welcome people warmly, and tell them where they are.

一群客人剛抵達你的學校——也許是別縣市的姊妹校，也許是來自國外的訪客。老師轉向你說：「你來當導覽員。」一個好導覽員做的第一件事很簡單：熱情地歡迎大家，並告訴他們，他們來到了什麼地方。

2

Key Words 關鍵字 · 先學會這幾個字

Learn these six words first — you will use them in your welcome.

Key words · 關鍵字
  • welcome(v.)to greet a guest in a friendly way · 歡迎、迎接
  • hometown(n.)the town you are from · 家鄉
  • named after(phr.)given the name of a person or thing · 以…命名
  • title(n.)an honour or rank given to a person · 封號、頭銜
  • admiral(n.)a very senior naval commander · 水師提督、海軍上將
  • proud of(phr.)pleased about something good · 以…為榮
🎙 Hear a key sentence · 聽一句例句

“Welcome to my hometown. I am proud to be your guide today.”

3

The Guide's Lines 導覽稿 · 跟著唸

Here is the welcome a Taibao guide might give. Read it once with your eyes, press 🔊 Listen, then say it out loud yourself.

🎙 Guide's script · 導覽稿

“Good morning, and welcome to Taibao! My name is _____, and I am a student at Taibao Junior High School. Our town has an unusual name. It is named after a famous admiral, Wang De-lu, who lived more than two hundred years ago. The emperor gave him a special title — ‘Grand Guardian’ — and our town took its name from that title. I am proud to be from Taibao. Let me show you around!”

「早安，歡迎來到太保！我的名字是 _____，我是太保國中的學生。我們的家鄉有個不尋常的名字。它以一位有名的提督——王得祿——命名，他生活在兩百多年前。皇帝賜給他一個特別的封號『太子太保』，我們的小鎮就以這個封號為名。我以身為太保人為榮。讓我帶你四處看看吧！」

4

Your Turn 換你說 · 把空格填成你自己的

Now make the welcome yours. Fill in the blanks out loud, using your own name. Practise it three times — until you do not need to look.

✍ Say it with your own words · 用你自己的話說

“Good morning, and welcome to Taibao! My name is ______. I am a student at Taibao Junior High. Our town is named after an admiral named Wang De-lu. I am proud to be from here. Let me show you around!”

小提示：說的時候看著對方的眼睛、微笑、慢慢說。導覽員的英文，不必完美——要清楚、要friendly。

Quick Check

Did You Get It?

小測驗：選出最適合的英文句子
1. A guest arrives. What is the best way to begin?
客人抵達了，最好的開場是哪一句？
Correct: A. A good guide opens by greeting the guest and welcoming them warmly. 好的導覽員會先問候、熱情地歡迎客人。
2. Our town is named after a person. Who?
我們的家鄉以一個人命名。是誰？
Correct: B. Taibao is named after the admiral Wang De-lu’s honorary title. 太保以提督王得祿的榮銜命名。
3. Which word means “the town you are from”?
哪個字是「你來自的家鄉」？
Correct: C — “hometown.” Your hometown is the town you come from. hometown 就是你來自的家鄉。
4. You want to say you feel good about being from Taibao. Which sentence works?
你想表達「以身為太保人為榮」，哪一句對？
Correct: A. “I am proud of / proud to be…” shows you feel good about something. 「proud of / proud to be」表示以某事為榮。

You just gave your first lines as a guide. The town is yours to show — in two languages. 你剛剛說出了身為導覽員的第一句話。這座家鄉，由你來介紹——用兩種語言。
