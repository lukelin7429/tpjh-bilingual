Educating the Whole Person 全人教育，終生學習

Rooted in our town, with our eyes on the world. 立足鄉土，胸懷世界。

Principal · 校長

Hsu Wen-cheng 徐文成 校長

Appointed August 2024 · 民國 113 年 8 月就任

Education — B.Sc. in Chemistry, National Kaohsiung Normal University; M.Sc., Institute of Chemistry, National Tsing Hua University.
學歷：高雄師範大學化學系、清華大學化學研究所

Experience — Homeroom teacher, Sanmin J.H.S. (New Taipei); Director, Chiahsin J.H.S.; Principal, Guogou J.H.S.; Principal, Liujia J.H.S. (Chiayi County).
經歷：蘆洲三民中學導師、嘉新國中主任、過溝國中校長、六嘉國中校長

Education for the whole person, and learning for a whole life. We want children of humanity and of science — rooted in their own town, with their eyes on the world. 全人教育，終生學習。我們要培養人文與科技並重的孩子——立足鄉土，胸懷世界。

To the families of Taibao, and to friends who find this page from further away — welcome.

致太保的家庭，以及從更遠的地方找到這一頁的朋友——歡迎你們。

I came to education by way of chemistry — a discipline that teaches you to test what you believe, and to respect what the evidence says. I have carried that habit through a homeroom in New Taipei and the principal's office of three Chiayi junior highs, and I bring it now to Taibao: the belief that a school should be a place of careful, joyful, lifelong learning.

我是從化學走進教育的——那是一門教你檢驗自己所相信的、並尊重證據所說的學問。我帶著這個習慣，走過新北的一間導師班、走過嘉義三所國中的校長室，如今把它帶到太保：相信學校應當是一個用心、喜悅、終生學習的地方。

I hold to a simple idea: the school exists for the student, and the student begins with health. From a strong, healthy body we build outward — to many kinds of intelligence, to good character, to an eye for what is beautiful and well made. And because Chiayi asks every child to grow good habits, to love their homeland, and to reach the world, we treat English not as a distant subject but as one of the doors through which our students will walk out to meet it.

我堅持一個簡單的想法：學校以學生為本，學生以健康為本。從健康強壯的身體向外建立——通往多元智慧、良好品格、以及欣賞美與「做得好」的眼光。而因為嘉義要每個孩子養成好習慣、愛鄉土、並且接國際，我們不把英語當成一個遙遠的科目，而是把它當作學生將要走出去、與世界相見的其中一道門。

Our students have already shown what that looks like — standing up to tell a story in English, hosting visitors from beyond Chiayi, carrying our name onto the wrestling mat at the national games. My task, and my colleagues’, is to keep widening the doors and then to stand back and watch them walk through.

我們的學生已經讓人看見那是什麼模樣——站起來用英文說一個故事、接待嘉義以外來的訪客、在全國運動會的角力墊上扛起我們的校名。我和同事們的任務，是把這些門開得更寬，然後退後一步，看著他們走過去。
Educational Philosophy

What I Hold To

我的教育理念
01

The Whole Person 全人教育

Humanity and science, side by side. We develop the whole child — many intelligences, not one ranking — so each student finds the door that opens for them.

人文與科技並重。我們發展完整的孩子——多元智慧，而非單一排名——讓每個學生都能找到為自己打開的那扇門。

02

Student-Centred, Health-First 以學生為本，以健康為本

The school exists for the student, and the student begins with health. In a campus of care, a young person grows strong enough to learn everything else.

學校以學生為本，學生以健康為本。在一個充滿關懷的校園裡，年輕人長得夠強壯，才學得動其他的一切。

03

Character, Eyes on the World 達人達德，胸懷世界

Family, school, and community together raise students of good character — who love their home, care for society, and reach out to the world in more than one language.

結合家庭、學校與社區，培養品格兼備的學生——愛護家人、關心社會、並用不只一種語言向世界伸出手。
